# Simple Way to Create Notification Icons

## Easiest Method (No Tools Needed):

Since you already have your dark icon, here's the SIMPLEST way:

### Option 1: Use This Simple Website
1. Go to: https://romannurik.github.io/AndroidAssetStudio/icons-notification.html
2. Click "Image" tab
3. Upload your dark icon (from AppIcons folder)
4. It will automatically convert it to white/transparent
5. Click "Download ZIP"
6. Extract the ZIP
7. Copy the `res/drawable-*` folders to your project

### Option 2: Use Your Dark Icon (Temporary)
We can use your dark icon temporarily - Android will make it work, just might not look perfect.

### Option 3: I Can Create Simple White Icons
If you want, I can create simple white outline versions of your hexagon icon for notifications.
